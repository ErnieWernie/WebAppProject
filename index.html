5 Essential Cybersecurity Practices Every Small Business Should Implement

It is expected that cybercrime will cost the world around $10.5 trillion(!!!!) USD annually by 2025! This cybercrime is not limited to large scale companies, anyone could be targeted by these criminals! That's why I believe that it is essential for small businesses to create backups for all data, implement strong and unique passwords, keep all their software updated, have multi factor authentication and should educate all employees on the severity of being cyber safe. This way even the smallest of mom and pop shops will be able to thrive without a major setback.

Understanding the Dark Web: The Good and The Bad

When I speak on the Dark Web, the people around me usually get frightened or are appalled at the thought of it. Why? Is it really that horrible? Are there horrors lying beyond each click of our mouse? The Dark Web carries the connotation of guns, drugs, violence and/or other depraved events. For those who are not aware, the Dark Web is encrypted online content that will not and can not be reached by our traditional search engines. It requires a browser such as TOR that provides the user with anonymity. This would give the chance for people in heavily monitored areas of the world to be in contact with the outside!
